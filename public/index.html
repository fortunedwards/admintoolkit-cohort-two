6-Week Course Structure

Week 1: Google Meet + Google Calendar

Master virtual meeting coordination and scheduling management for optimal productivity and communication.

Week 2: Google Forms + Gmail

Create powerful online surveys and questionnaires while mastering professional email management and communication strategies.

Week 3: Google Drive + Google Docs

Learn cloud file management with advanced sharing permissions and professional document creation with collaborative editing.

Week 4: Trello + Google Keep

Implement project management workflows with Kanban boards and organize thoughts with digital note-taking and idea management systems.

Week 5: Google Sheets

Create powerful spreadsheets, data analysis, formulas, charts, and automated reporting for business insights.

workspace_premium

Week 6: Graduation Week

Final assessment, project completion, and certification for Administrative Toolkit mastery.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the training? expand_more

6 weeks total: 5 weeks covering essential productivity tools (Google Meet+Calendar, Forms+Gmail, Drive+Docs, Trello+Keep, Sheets) plus a final graduation/certification week.

What if I miss a class? expand_more

All course materials remain accessible throughout the program. You can review previous weeks' content at any time. Consistent absences may lead to removal from the program to maintain group progress.

Who is eligible? expand_more

Open to anyone wanting to build administrative skills. No prior experience needed, just willingness to learn and commit.
